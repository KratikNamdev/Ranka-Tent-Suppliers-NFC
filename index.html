About Ranka Tent Suppliers

Established in 1998, Ranka Tent Suppliers is a trusted trader, wholesaler, and distributor of high-quality event and household products, including Air Coolers, Wedding Furniture, Wooden Sofa Sets, Red Printed Non-Woven Carpets, Raj Mist Fans, Wooden Swings (Jhoolas), Wooden White Folding Tables, as well as Decoration and Arrangement Services. We are committed to fulfilling our customers’ expectations by delivering products of excellent quality and ensuring complete satisfaction. Guided by ethical business practices and transparent dealings, we strive to maintain long-lasting and healthy relationships with our clients. Our success story is deeply rooted in the vision and guidance of Mr. Paras Ranka, whose unwavering support has been instrumental in achieving remarkable growth and establishing our presence in today’s competitive market.
